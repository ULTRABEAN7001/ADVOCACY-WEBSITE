Health Poverty

Our group has chosen this specific topic since we are all aware that poverty is one of the biggest issues that plagues the world. We chose this because we want to battle poverty along with our fellow Filipinos affected by it. Most issues are the result of poverty for example crime rates increasing and minor outbreaks of diseases. Analyzing the interconnected nature of poverty and considering other factors such as maintaining the solution to poverty and its repercussions demonstrates a holistic approach to problem-solving. By delving into the complexities surrounding poverty, our group will use the values of service, prudence, and generosity to contribute valuable insights that could pave the way for more effective and sustainable solutions.

"Empower change: lend a hand, lift a spirit."

What are our goals?

  • Improve the quality of life of the people affected by health poverty, by providing support as a country , community and filipino citizens we can show our support by providing any kind of help towards those who are affected by health poverty.
  • Enhance the Philippine health situation through a promotional website. By providing information on the situation and to spread awareness about the current problems in the Philippines health care.
  • Improve the knowledge of healthcare professionals about the current health problems of Filipinos. The website aims to increase the information and knowledge of healthcare professionals by providing credible information about the life and health of Filipinos affected by poverty.
  • Reduce Filipinos' health problems .With the information given by the website and the knowledge we have in the current Filipino healthcare , we can find differences and implement improvements towards the current Filipino healthcare. In addition to providing information, the website should be able to influence healthcare professionals and professors to expand their skills in providing help to the people affected by Health poverty.
